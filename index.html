Ryan O'Connor

Founder and Owner of Newfound Potential Consulting, or 'NPC'

I am a connisseur and afficinado of video games, a lover of soccer, and owner of a professional coaching and business consulting company.

Career and Education

My career was not driven by my education, in fact, I took quite the opposite route to build my career. Having dropped out of college at the age of 21 years old, I ended up just starting to work and building a professional toolkit that allowed me to excel in my career. This was the basis for creating NPC. My education, equally important to me, came second, but when it was the right time in my life for it.

Failures and Successes

  • Successes

    • Captained and lead my work's IT Dragonboat team to Second Place victory in 2018 and 2019 consecutively.
    • Obtained my first promotion in 2021, went from a Level II Cardiovascular Imaging Systems system administrator to a Level III.
    • Founded Newfound Potential Consulting, LLC (or 'NPC' for short) on my Dad's birthday, in January 2022.
